Recipe for Adobo Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • 3cups of buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon slt
  • 1 teaspoon hot red pepper sauce
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoonground black pepper
  • 6 cups of canola oil

      • Procedure

      1. Place chicken in a large food storage bag.Add buttermilk,salt and hot red pepper sauce.seal bagand refrigerate over night
      2. in a large plastic bag combine flour,garlicpowder,onion powder and pepper and to mix.Add a few pieces of chicken at a time to bag and shake to coat.Placechicken on waxed paper
      3. Heat canola oil
      4. In batches ,fry chicken 12 to 14 minutes,drain chicken on paper towels and keep warm in oven
        5. Go to home
