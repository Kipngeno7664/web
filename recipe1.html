Recipe For Marinated Lamb Chops

Ingredients

  • 3Kgs of lamb chops
  • 4 table teaspoonful of cooking oil
  • 2 Cups of water
  • 1 teaspoonful of salt
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 3 Bulb onions
  • Parsley
  • 2 Caspium
  • 1 garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomato paste

Procedure

  1. Put lamb chops in a colander and wash them
  2. Put the lamb chops in a cooking pan and simmer on top of a gas for fifteen minutes until it drys
  3. saute the lamb chops by basting in cooking oil until they turn golden brown
  4. Chop the ginger and garlic and them to the meat as you stir continuously
  5. Add the onions after chopping them and fry for one minute
  6. Dice the tomato using a dicer and add to the lamb chops then simmer for three minutes until they turn soft and tender into a paste
  7. Add the tomato paste and salt to season the food
  8. Stir the food while adding vinegar and soy sauce to give it flavour
  9. Add chopped parsley and caspium to garnish the food then fry for three minutes
